Unlock the Power of a Connected Fleet with Detroit Connect Portal

Detroit Connect® is a comprehensive suite of connected vehicle services designed to help fleets operate smarter and more efficiently. It provides real-time data, remote diagnostics, safety insights, and maintenance tools—all accessible through a centralized portal. With Detroit Connect, fleet managers can make informed decisions, reduce downtime, and optimize performance across their operations.

Smarter Operations Start with Connectivity

Our suite of Detroit Connect services turn vehicle data and signals into actionable insights to help your fleet operate smarter, safer, and more efficiently. From optimizing fleet service and maintenance schedules to managing vehicles remotely to improving driver behavior and performance, our connected solutions give you the tools and confidence to make informed decisions that keep your trucks performing at their best.

Designed to Drive Relevant Results

  • Improve fleet visibility and operation control with real-time tracking and remote locking capabilities.
  • Optimize service planning and minimize downtime through continuous vehicle health monitoring and over-the-air updates.
  • Enhance driver performance and coaching with connected insights into driving behavior and safety events.
  • Stay ahead of unplanned downtime with remote fault severity analysis and remote vehicle access.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are connected vehicle services?

Connected vehicle services use vehicle data and remote connectivity to deliver diagnostics, safety insights, and fleet management tools.

Why should I invest in connected services for my fleet?

They help reduce downtime, improve safety, and give fleet managers actionable insights to operate more efficiently.

What vehicles are eligible for Detroit Connect services?

Eligibility depends on vehicle configuration and subscription. Sign in or contact your dealer to confirm coverage for your fleet.

How do I access and use Detroit Connect services?

Sign in with your DTNA Portal ID to access the Detroit Connect Portal and available connected services.

Can I manage multiple vehicles or fleets from one account?

Yes — the portal is designed to support fleet-level visibility and management across eligible vehicles.

How is my vehicle data protected?

Detroit Connect follows DTNA data protection practices to help keep fleet and vehicle information secure.

How long are service terms?

Service terms vary by subscription package. Check your account or dealer for plan-specific details.

Where can I purchase/renew subscriptions?

Subscriptions and renewals are available through your dealer and eligible online account flows.

What happens if/when my subscription expires?

Access to connected features may end when a subscription expires until it is renewed.

Where can I find more information about Detroit Connect services?

Sign in for full service details, or contact your local dealership for guidance.

Who can I contact for technical issues/troubleshooting?

Contact customer support through the portal or your local dealership for technical assistance.

How do I cancel my connectivity services?

Cancellation options depend on your subscription terms. Contact your dealer or account support to proceed.
